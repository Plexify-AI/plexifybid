Golden Triangle Business Improvement District (BID)
Annual Report — 2024

Executive Summary
In 2024, the Golden Triangle BID focused on streetscape maintenance, public safety coordination, marketing and events, and capital planning to support property owners and business vitality.

Assessment Collections
- Total assessments billed (FY 2024): $2,440,000
- Total assessments collected (FY 2024): $2,300,000
- Assessment collection rate: 94.2%

Capital Projects
- Streetscape improvements: sidewalk repairs, tree well enhancements, and litter abatement.
- Wayfinding signage: design and installation planning to improve district navigation.

Financial Summary (FY 2024)
- Revenue: $2.44M assessments billed; $2.30M collected.
- Expenses: operations, safety coordination, cleaning, and marketing.
- Reserves: maintained for planned capital enhancements.

Board of Directors (2024)
- Chair: Alex Morgan (Term: 2023–2025)
- Vice Chair: Priya Shah (Term: 2024–2026)
- Treasurer: Daniel Kim (Term: 2022–2024)

2025 Priorities
- Improve assessment collections and delinquency follow-up.
- Launch wayfinding signage program.
- Expand stakeholder engagement with property owners and the board.
